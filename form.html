PAKHWAJ-a brief information

The pakhwaj is a barrel-shaped, two-headed drum, originating from the indian subcontinent, the oldest version of double sided drums and its descendants are Mridangam of Southern India and kendang of Maritime Southeast Asia and other south Asian double-headed drums.
